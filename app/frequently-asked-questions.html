Frequently Asked Questions

  • Who is the book designed for?

    Our book is designed for children aged 2 to 8 but is also frequently bought as a special keepsake for new borns.

  • What format is the book?

    The book is 26cm wide by 23cm tall. It is digitally printed on high quality, thick paper.

  • How much do the books cost?

    Our books are £19.99 each, which includes the customised book and bright gift packaging. You will also receive a key code to access your online 'my world', containing bonus content and quizzes.

  • Where do you deliver to?

    We deliver to all countries. We will let you know the expected delivery date for your location during the ordering process.

  • How long does it take to arrive?

    Uk orders typically take 7-10 days and overseas orders 14-21 days.

  • How is the book packaged?

    Each book is sent in a bright red book wrap and can be addressed directly to the child, so that the excitement starts as soon as they first receive it!

  • What is my world?

    Each book contains a unique code that you can use to unlock your very own 'my world' of learning online. Here you can continue your story where you left off or take a quiz to see how much you learned and can remember!

  • Have another question?

    If you have any other questions, please feel free to contact us and we will get right back to you.
