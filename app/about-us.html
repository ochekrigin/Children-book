About us

Our books are designed to create magical experiences using a unique blend of print and digital technology that kids will treasure. Children have great fun receiving and reading their personalised books and then continue the enjoyment online using the book as a special key to unlock a Wondrous World of learning.

Our debut book, Wondrous World, is one of the most beautifully illustrated and conceived bespoke children's books, where the letters in a chosen child's name become part of the adventure. This exquisite keepsake makes a really personal gift to fire mini imaginations now and beyond.

The team

MIKE & ANGELICA BRITTAIN

The Wondrous World book is brought to you by Mike & Angelica, a husband and wife team with a real passion for bringing back the magic of books and engaging children through learning and reading. Building on the success of their Christmas letter service, Magical Santa, they decided to create the most wonderful book for children to receive ever, and set out to build a dream team that could help bring their vision to life.

Read more about how we made our book on our blog

SARAH KHAN, AUTHOR

Sarah has been in children's publishing for all her working life, writing and editing books about everything from gerbils to gene therapy. She also has a puzzle column in the Telegraph Weekend supplement. Wondrous World is her first work of fiction. She enjoys spending time with her husband and family, and wandering around bookshops in the Cotswolds.

Find Sarah on Twitter at @sarah1k

ANDREA SANZ, ILLUSTRATOR

Andrea started out as a Psychologist, but soon realised that her passion lay in children's illustration. She travelled from Madrid to Scotland to pursue a course in Digital design before continuing her studies at an illustration college in Spain. These days you'll find her hard at work in her studios in Madrid, dreaming about new ways to spark the imagination of children through her unique hand-drawn illustration style.

See her portfolio at www.behance.net/andreasanz
